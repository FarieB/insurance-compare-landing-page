Key Features

Feature 1

The webpage has three dropdowns. First the customer selects the class of insurance "select insurance type", that is either life or non life insurance.

Feature 2

The customer the selects the product from the list that becomes available after select the class of insurance.

Feature 3

A list a companies providing the product selected above becomes available to choose from

Feature 4

A comparison result becomes available showing company name, product type, premium, coverage and terms and conditions summarized.
